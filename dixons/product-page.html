        Product information
        Top features:

        - QuickWash delivers brilliant results fast

        - ecobubble technology cleans at lower temperatures

        - AddWash door lets you quickly add anything during the wash

        - Sensor washing for optimum washing results

        - Smart control lets you check and monitor the cycle from your smartphone

        QuickWash

        Thanks to innovative QuickDrive technology, this Samsung QuickDrive WW10M86DQOA Washing Machine reduces the time of the wash cycle without compromising on cleaning performance.

        By creating a fast water flow that covers the entire drum, detergent penetrates deep into fabrics for a thorough wash.

        ecobubble technology

        You can wash at lower temperatures thanks to ecobubble technology. It works by mixing air, water, and detergent to create bubbles that penetrate your clothes faster. It's better for the environment and helps you save on your bills.

        AddWash door

        If you've missed an item, want to add extra softner, or simply pop in a hand-washed garment for final rinsing, you can use the AddWash door. The small opening in the main door can be opened at any time in the cycle when the temperature is below 50°C.

        Sensor washing

        The Auto Optimal Wash uses four sensors to adjust the time and water depending on the weight of the load, ensuring water and energy aren't wasted. During the wash cycle it assesses the level of soiling and will add more detergent and time towards the end of the cycle if necessary.

        Smart control

        Check and monitor the laundry cycle from anywhere with the smartphone app for Android and iOS. You can control the cycle, select laundry recipes, and monitor energy performance and detergent levels from your sofa, office, or when out and about.

        Sarah

        Templecombe
        +
        I have really fallen in love with this washing machine.
        A washing machine is an essential for my family and everything quickly grinds to a halt without a working machine in the house. Having had several washing machines in the last few years and althought I thought I had a good solid machine from a very well respected brand that did the job this machine is so much more - Samsung says it's quick and it is -the Quick Drive and Eco Bubble deliver exceptional results with really clean washing at half the time I am used to . It is also has a much simpler structure of recommend weights with a good deal of the washing options taking 10 kg. Great for a family of 4.
        It really is so quiet it can sit easily within open plan living and not be heard except for water fills which is amazing - there is no vibration so apart from the flashing display so you wouldn't know it was on.
        I always thought the Add Wash function was a bit of a gimmick but it came into its own straight away and was so much better than having to hope the water level in my old machine was low enough to pause the machine and open the whole door.
        For me the measure of a good machine is its wool wash - just because so many of my work clothes require a careful wash and this was where the washing machine excelled - beautifully clean and perfectly spun so no shrinkage and no stretch being able to tailor the function and reduce the temperature and spin was also useful to be able to do.
        This is a machine that you have to invest a little time in getting to know but so worthwhile if you want to get the best out of it. Using functions like auto dosing is going to save you money on detergent and keep your clothes and machine in optimum condition.
        I do want control over my washing so being able to vary temperature is important but the sensor option also takes all the hassle away if you want a simple life so the best of both worlds.
        Overall the look of the machine is lovely too - having always stuck to white the graphite gives a really contemporary feel. The big porthole is easy to load from and the smoked glass door means I don't have to share my underwear whizzing around with anyone else. The light is a useful feature although the shadows do give your washing a dappled effect which was a bit of a surprise until I worked out where it came from.
        −
        For a 10 kg washing machine it's still the same size as my old 8 kg but measure carefully !
        However, lovely as this machine is it does need to be right for you. Automatic dosing is great if you are a one washing liquid household otherwise it is a case of not using that function if you swap from wool liquid to liquid for colours and another for liquid for whites. Again if you use capsules or powder then you won't use the automatic dosing system.
        Its important that you check that you have the right phone in order to access the Samsung Connect App that allows Smart Control
